Vocabulary Definitions

HTML Meta Tags

<meta> for charset

Specifies the character encoding for the HTML document.

<meta> for keywords

Provides keywords for search engines to index the page.

<meta> for viewport

Controls the layout of the page on different devices and screen sizes.

<meta> for refresh

Specifies a time interval for the page to refresh automatically.

<meta> for author

Defines the author of the webpage.

Cybersecurity Concepts

Confidentiality (AIC Triad)

Ensuring that information is not accessed by unauthorized parties.

Integrity (AIC Triad)

Maintaining the accuracy and completeness of data.

Availability (AIC Triad)

Ensuring that information is accessible to authorized users when needed.

White Hat Hacker

A cybersecurity expert who uses hacking techniques for ethical purposes, like improving security.

Black Hat Hacker

An individual who uses hacking for malicious purposes, such as stealing data.

Project Management

Requirements Traceability

The ability to link requirements to their origins and track them throughout the project lifecycle.

Iron Triangle

A project management model that balances three constraints: scope, time, and cost.
